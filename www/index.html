N2: A Packer's Lament

The bloated frame, a memory now faint,
Replaced by whispers, of a new constraint.
Three bits upfront, the ttt's decree,
Dictate the form of what's to come, you see.
A zero first, then four, a fleeting sign,
For integers, in `zigzag-value`'s shrine.
No wasted sign, the negative embraced,
A twisted path, efficiently traced.

But if a one, the `ttt` should betray,
The varint stretches, steals another day.
Seven more bits, a hungry, grasping hand,
`1xxxx`, then `0xxxxxxx`, across the land
Of bytes consumed, a chain that pulls and bites,
Until the final zero dims the lights.

Then comes the `decimal`, a peculiar pair,
Two varints born, a burden they must bear.
The first, `zigzag-power`, sharp and keen,
The second, `base`, a fuller, fatter scene.
Eight bits it claims, to start its solemn quest,
No `ttt` to guide, it puts the rule to test.
`0xxxxxxx`, or `1xxxxxxx`'s sprawl,
A dual dance, responding to the call.

For `string` and `binary`, a simple `length` defined,
The measure told, the data left behind.
The `list` and `map`, their children to declare,
A varint count, how many souls are there.

The `pointer` leaps, an `offset` in the gloom,
To find its mark within the crowded room.
And `refs` point back, an `index` sharp and cold,
To stories that have previously been told.
The sacred `zero`, `null`'s eternal void,
Then `one` for `true`, a verity enjoyed.
And `two` for `false`, the stark negation's chill,
Built-in constants, standing stark and still.

So flows the stream, a current dense and deep,
Where types are bound, and secrets tightly sleep.
Each packet lean, a specter, swift and grim,
Born of the `ttt`, and the Optimizer's whim.
